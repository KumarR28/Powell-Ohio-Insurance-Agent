Auto Insurance

Malhotra & Associates has been developing an insurance community to improve the insurance coverage extended to you for years. We are a top providers of customer support and representation in Ohio insurance. We are very proud of the insurance services we can offer you, especially our comprehensive Ohio car insurance policies .

Ohio auto insurance may be one of the most common lines of insurance, but that doesn’t mean you should take shortcuts when weighing your options. We believe that each customer should know and understand all of their Ohio car insurance options when making this important decision. Our well-informed agents always do their best to simplify the Ohio auto insurance selection process for you, and help you make the right decision that’s best for you.

We offer the following options for your Ohio car insurance:

    Liability - insurance for property damage and injuries to others caused by you while operating your vehicle.

    Medical or Personal Injury Protection - covers treatment of the driver and passengers of the policyholder’s car in the event of an accident.

    Collision - insures the policyholder from loss during a collision with another vehicle or object.

    Comprehensive - protection for the policyholder’s car from damage and loss resulting from incidents other than collision.

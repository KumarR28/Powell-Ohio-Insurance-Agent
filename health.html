Health Insurance

The very best Ohio health insurance is available at competitive rates through Malhotra & Associates.

You never know when an accident may occur or a health issue will strike, so whether you’re located in Columbus or Cleveland, Cincinnati, health insurance should certainly be a priority. Malhotra & Associates offers the most thorough and amazing coverage on individual health insurance in Ohio.

If you are self-employed or your employer does not offer health coverage, you probably need to secure an affordable Ohio health insurancepolicy. Malhotra & Associates are available to answer all of your health insurance questions.

Our health insurance policies are easily tailored to match your unique needs, and most often provide coverage for:

    Doctors visits

    Prescriptions

    Specialized care

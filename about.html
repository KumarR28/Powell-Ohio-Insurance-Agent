About Us - Bob Malhotra

Hi there, I'm Bob Malhotra of Malhotra & Associates. My family and I have lived in the Central Ohio area for over 20 years, and our extensive experience in this area allows us to have a unique perspective on risk management for our clients.

Our boutique style services give us the flexibility to offer a wide range of risk management solutions and allow us to provide each of our clients with the personalized customer service attention that is a hallmark of Malhotra & Associates.

We offer a broad range of insurance coverage including Commercial, Auto, Umbrella, Health and Life Insurance. We represent some of the finest national and regional insurance carriers who value excellent claims and policy service.

Our goals are to help each of our clients establish thoughtful strategies for insurance protection, maximize client value, and provide access to world markets. Contact us today to learn more about us or to receive a free consultation.
